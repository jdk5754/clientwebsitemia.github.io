ALL ABOUT MIA

Mia Gomez Montero is a New York based theater maker, actor, singer, and dancer. A proud Jersey Girl, Mia earned her Bachelor's Degrees in English and Theatre from Penn State University.

In her youth, Mia gained experience in the arts, training at the Paper Mill Playhouse and New Jersey School of Ballet. Since then she has been seen regionally at venues like the Union County and MAYO Performing Arts Centers. In her time at Penn State she made her Centre Stage debut by swinging on for their production of Cabaret, despite having been the Assistant Director of the production. In addition to serving as the Assistant Director and Assistant Choreographer for 5 Centre Stage productions she later went on to play the role of Madeline in Ike Holter's Hit the Wall. She could not be more proud to be a Nittany Lion. We Are!

Mia's collegiate experience afforded her the opportunity to study at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art in London under the instruction of world-renowned tutors like Geoff Colman, Marina Calderone, and Tamsin Collison. Her experience abroad connected her with the Voice Over industry under the expertise of Richard Cotton, thus leading to her UK representation with The Voice People and a new passion that she adores.

When Mia isn't performing you can often find her curled up with a good book and a cup of tea, or simply spending time with the people she loves over some paella and sangria.
